Service routing

Choose the category that best describes the electrical request.

VoltLink organizes homeowner requests into practical service lanes. Each lane helps independent local providers understand the urgency, property context, likely tools, and next questions before they respond.

01

Urgent symptoms

Power loss, buzzing, warm devices, burning odor, storm-related issues, and breaker problems.

02

Planned work

New outlets, lighting, dedicated circuits, appliance hookups, and panel or wiring projects.

03

Safety review

Older home concerns, repeated issues, inspection needs, remodel checks, and visible risk questions.

What to include

Better request details lead to better provider conversations.

Location

City, ZIP, property type, and where the issue is inside or outside the home.

Urgency

Whether it is happening now, recurring, planned, or connected to a larger project.

Context

Photos, breaker labels, fixture details, access notes, and recent changes.

Verification

Ask the provider about license, insurance, scope, permits, and payment terms.
