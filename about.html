Home/About

A clearer first step for electrical service requests.

VoltLink helps homeowners organize electrical concerns and connect with independent local providers. We are not the contractor, and that distinction is central to how the service works.

Our story

Built for the moment when a homeowner needs a clear next step.

Electrical problems are stressful because the risk is real and the symptoms are not always easy to describe. A breaker trips, a room loses power, an outlet feels warm, or a remodel suddenly needs professional wiring input.

VoltLink was created around a simple idea: homeowners should be able to describe the issue once and get routed toward available local service providers without spending hours comparing unrelated websites.

Our role

Matching support without pretending to be the provider.

What we help with

  • Collect service category, location, urgency, and project notes.
  • Help connect homeowners with independent local providers when available.
  • Keep safety and verification reminders visible before hiring.

What we do not do

  • We do not perform inspections, estimates, repairs, or installations.
  • We do not supervise providers or guarantee their work.
  • We do not replace homeowner verification of license and insurance.

How matching starts

The request is shaped before a provider conversation begins.

A good provider conversation starts with the basics: what happened, where it happened, how urgent it is, and whether the request is repair, installation, wiring, panel, lighting, or inspection related.

Intake

Homeowner shares category, property location, urgency, and notes.

Routing

The request is organized around the type of local provider likely needed.

Provider contact

Independent providers may respond with next steps, availability, and scope questions.

Homeowner responsibility

The final hiring decision should be made carefully.

VoltLink can help start the connection, but the homeowner chooses the provider. Before authorizing work, confirm credentials, estimate details, permits, warranties, and payment terms directly with the independent provider.
