Cookie Policy

How cookies may support this site.

Effective date: July 8, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how cookies and similar technologies may be used on VoltLink Electrical Match, a free aggregator service that helps homeowners connect with independent local electrical service providers.

What Cookies Are Types We May Use Third Parties Your Controls Contact

1. What cookies and similar technologies are

Cookies are small files or identifiers stored on a browser or device. Similar technologies may include pixels, tags, scripts, local storage, session identifiers, analytics identifiers, and device signals. These tools can help websites operate, remember settings, measure performance, and understand how visitors use pages and forms.

2. Types of cookies we may use

Essential cookies

These support basic website operation, page loading, security, form submission, session handling, and prevention of misuse.

Functional cookies

These may remember preferences, form progress, user choices, or display settings that improve the visitor experience.

Analytics cookies

These help us understand page visits, traffic sources, device types, browser behavior, and how visitors interact with service request pages.

Marketing or routing cookies

These may help measure advertising performance, understand referral sources, prevent duplicate requests, or support service request routing.

3. How cookies support the aggregator model

Because this site helps route homeowner requests to independent providers, cookies and similar technologies may support request intake, fraud prevention, duplicate submission detection, analytics, marketing attribution, and operational reporting.

For example, cookies may help us understand which service pages are used before a homeowner submits a request, whether a form error occurs, or whether a visitor arrived from a particular referral source.

4. Third-party technologies

We may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, security, form processing, call tracking, advertising measurement, or request routing. These third parties may set or read their own cookies, pixels, or identifiers subject to their own policies.

Independent providers or routing partners that receive request information may also use their own technologies if you visit their websites or communicate with them directly.

5. Information cookies may collect

Cookies and similar technologies may collect or infer technical and usage information such as IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, pages viewed, time on page, referring URL, approximate location, interactions with forms, and advertising or analytics identifiers.

6. Managing cookies

Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or limit cookies through browser settings. You may also be able to use device settings, privacy extensions, or third-party opt-out tools to limit certain analytics or advertising technologies.

If you disable cookies, some parts of the site may not function as intended. Forms, security checks, analytics accuracy, preference settings, or request routing features may be affected.

7. Do Not Track and preference signals

Some browsers send "Do Not Track" or similar signals. Because there is no single industry standard for responding to all such signals, this site may not respond to every browser-based signal. We may respond to legally required preference signals where applicable.

8. Updates to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as technologies, providers, analytics tools, or legal requirements change. The effective date above indicates the latest version.

9. Contact

Questions about this Cookie Policy may be sent to support@voltlinkelectrical.com.
